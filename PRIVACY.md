# Privacy Policy

CodeWhisper is a macOS application that integrates with NSServices. Your privacy choices matter:

- **Clipboard/selection access:** CodeWhisper reads the selected text only when you explicitly trigger an action via right-click menu. It does not monitor clipboard in the background.
- **Local AI (Ollama / llama.cpp):** When configured with a local AI backend, your code never leaves your machine.
- **Cloud AI:** If you configure a cloud AI provider, your selected code is sent to that provider per their privacy policy. You control this setting.
- **No telemetry:** CodeWhisper does not collect usage statistics or phone home.
